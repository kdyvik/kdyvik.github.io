About FoodSpace

FoodSpace is a site where the best recipes for food and drinks are collected. Our site features a minimalism design, so its all about the recipes, and nothing else! The recipes on our site are carefully tried out by our staff before its posted on our site. The reason this page was created, was that there are very few websites that only excellent recipes. Thats why we wanted to make a place where people can find all the best recipes. Our collection features recipes from all kinds of chefs, aswell as user submitted content. If you want to submit a recipe, click on the contact-button and send us an email. Then well make your food or drink before we evaluate if its something that live up to our quality.
