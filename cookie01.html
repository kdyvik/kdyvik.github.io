Chocolate Oatmeal Cookies

28 small cookies

By Hedwig Matheney

  • Butter, 100 g
  • Sugar, 1.5 dl
  • Egg, 1 pcs.
  • Vanilla Sugar, 1 tsp.
  • Baking soda, 1 tsp.
  • Flour, 2.5 dl
  • Oats, 2 dl
  • Chocolate, 100g

Preheat your oven on 200 celcius.

Start by whipping your sugar and heatened butter into a cream, and then add the egg.

Then, add your baking soda, vanilla sugar and flour and stir it into a nice dough.

Chop up the chocolate and mix it into the dough with the oats.

Make balls out of the dough, and place them on a baking tray. Make sure to have some space between them so they dont grow into eachother while cooking. You can alter the size of the cookies, but this recipe will give you about 28 small ones.

Put the baking tray in the oven and watch the magic happen. When you have small size-cookies like i do, cook them for 10 minutes. You might have to leave them in the oven longer if you have larger cookies.
