Avocado and Egg Sandwich

1 portion

By Fannie Murray

  • Whole Wheat Bread, 2 slices.
  • Olive Oil
  • Avocado, 1 pcs.
  • Egg, 2 pcs.
  • Pine Nuts, 1 handfull
  • Salt and Pepper

Start with boiling your eggs for 8 minutes. This will give the eggs a creamy but still hard yolk. While you do this, fry your slices of bread in a little bit of olive oil, until the bread is semi-toasted.

Peel your avocados and slice them into medium pieces. When the bread is finished, place the avocados on it. Afterwars slice your eggs into 3 pieces each, and take the parts on top of the avocado.

Finish your toast with a handfull of pine nuts and some salt and pepper, to enhance the other flavors. Now its ready to be served!
