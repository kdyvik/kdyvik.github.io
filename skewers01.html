Chicken Skewers

10 pcs.

By Philip Doyle

  • Chicken, 400g
  • Soy Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • Sea Salt
  • Chili, 1/2 pcs.
  • Lime, 1/2 pcs.
  • Shallot, 3 pcs.
  • Yellow Pepper, 1 pcs.
  • Squash, 1/2 pcs.
  • Skewer sticks

Start by preheating your grill. It is possible to use a frying pan aswell, but a grill gives you the best flavor.

Cut your chiken in squares and put it into a bath of the juice of half a lime, black pepper, sea salt, some soy sauce and finely cut chili. While this sauce is going into your chicken, cut your other ingredients to small sqares. Make sure that the sizes of the different vegetables are the same, so you get an equal amount of each item. After 15 minutes, place squares of your chicken and vegetables on top of eachother in order.

When you have prepared your skewers, place them on your grill until the chicken is a little burned on one side (3 min) before you flip it around until every side is covered. Then your skewers should be ready. It is recommended that you try to cut one of the chicken-pieces open to check if it is done before serving. If its not, take another round on the grill.
