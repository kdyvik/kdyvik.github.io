Pizza

4 portions

By Thomas Ortiz

    Dough:

  • Water, 3 dl
  • Fresh Yeast, 1/2 pack
  • Olive Oil, 2 tbsp.
  • Salt, 1/2 tsp.
  • Flour, 7 dl

    • Sauce:

  • Garlic Clove, 2 pcs.
  • Shallot, 2 pcs.
  • Olive Oil, 2 tbsp.
  • Canned Tomatoes, 2 packs
  • Fresh Basil
  • Salt, 1/2 tsp.
  • Pepper, 1/2 tsp.

    • Get your favorite topping.

Preheat the oven to 200 degrees Celsius.

Dough:

1) Heat the water to body temperature.

2) Stir yeast into the water and add oil, flour and salt.

3) Bring the dough well together and let it rise to double size.

4) Use a rolling pin to shape the pizza dough to your preffered size. If you are making larger quantities of pizza, you should consider shaping it in a square to fill your baking sheet.

Sauce:

1) Bring your canned tomatoes into a finely ground sieve, and let them drain.

2) Chop shallots and garlic, and fry it in olive oil until the shallot is shiny. Then add your canned tomaatoes and some finely chopped fresh basil.

3) Let the sauce boil for 20 minutes, and add the salt and pepper if necessary.

When this is done you can start to fill your pizza. Start with spreading your sauce over the dough. Afterwars take your selecection of cheese on top of it, before you add the rest of your topping. On the photo, I have used goat cheese, red and green peppers, red onion and olives.

After you have filled your delicious pizza, put it in the oven for 10-14 minutes. Make sure the edges are a little crisp for the best experience.
