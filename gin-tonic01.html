Gin and Tonic

By Steven Williams

  • Gin, 50ml
  • Tonic Water, 200ml
  • Ice Cubes
  • Whole Black Pepper
  • Lemon/Lime

1) Fill your glass up with ice.

2) Add your favorite gin.

3) Add some black pepper or a little squeeze of lemon or lime.

4) Pour in the tonic water.

5) Add some lemon peel for garnish, and serve.
